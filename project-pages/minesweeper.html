MINESWEEPER

After playing Minesweeper on the weekly for as long as I can remember, I finally decided to make it. It's in a relatively rough state visually, but the ultimate goal is to translate it to JavaScript to embed it here, or make (my first) app.

In the process of making it, I did a lot of Minesweeper related research and learned a few things:

  • Minesweeper implements a buffer behind the scenes around the 2D matrix in order to create no special cases in checking squares.
  • The cascading algorithm is fairly simple.
  • I wanted to make it a guess-free version (I hate when there's only two squares and I guess wrong) but it may actually be less fun that way?
  • There was a campaign to ban the mine version of the game, that offered a flower edition.

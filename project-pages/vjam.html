VECTORJAM

SARAH LAWRENCE COLLEGE | QUANTUM COMPUTING

Fall 2016

Conference project for Quantum Computing class. Completed with classmate, Avinoam, and titled after our "brand" we've created, JAM. During our summer research project it stood for "Juliet Avinoam Mike" but this time it was "Juliet Avinoam Math". vectorJAM is a visualizer for linear algebra concepts we learned in the class. It shows the interaction of a matrix on a vector, along with displaying the eigenvalues, eigenvectors, and product vectors. It supports complex matrices, as well, and visualizes in the complex plane.

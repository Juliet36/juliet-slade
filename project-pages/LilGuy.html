LIL GUY

SARAH LAWRENCE COLLEGE | OUR WORLD, OTHER WORLDS

Spring 2017

I've always thought about walking on the ceiling and the challenges and advantages it would present, and was able to explore it in the classroom in two courses this year. Early on, in discussions with friends, Lil Guy was created. A small person who lives on the ceiling, he embodies a child-like imagination. Every person who I bring this up with has some sort of "what if I walked on the ceiling" experiences, and lots of interesting ceiling ideas, thoughts, and questions about the physics of it all.

This book includes words to describe what Lil Guy is experiencing, as he doesn't see things in the same context as we do. The images provide an insight to the reader of what's really going on.
